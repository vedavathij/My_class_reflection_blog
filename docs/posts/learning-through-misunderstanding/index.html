Learning through my misunderstanding : Reflection on Unit 1

Since I was a kid, I believed all the data and information I see and encounter are all correct. That’s why may be,I never dared to question anything. The difference between information, data, knowledge and how visuals impacts on topic or how it conveys the idea, they always felt very confusing to me and at the same time I often misunderstood these topics. In the first unit of our ‘Information science’ course, we were questioned on these topics. My mind went blank. I began to think “does these all topis have different meaning? does it make large difference to the meaning they hold?”, instead of giving answer, my mind was whispering the questions back to me. At first it felt little complicated to understand the meaning and difference of information, data, knowledge and wisom. Then we were introduced to a new topic which actually made sense to me for all the difference and how they actually works. it’s called “DIKW pyramid”. And also we learned about how a misinformation can lead to consequences, to create right type of visuals and graphs based on topic and viewers. All these learning has made lot of difference in my daily life because I have started to notice and observe even tiny details of posters and contains I come across in everyday life. Now, instead of accepting everything, I question it , analyze it, try to understand the actual meaning behind the content and what they are trying to say.
